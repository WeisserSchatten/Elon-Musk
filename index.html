Elon Reeve Musk

Biography

Elon Reeve Musk (родился 28 июня 1971 года) является американским бизнес-магнатом, инвестором, инженером из Южной Африки.Он является основателем, генеральным директором и техническим директором SpaceX, генеральным директором и разработчиком продуктов Tesla Inc, сопредседателем OpenAI, и основатель и генеральный директор Neuralink. Мusk также является соучредителем и бывшим председателем SolarCity, соучредителем Zip2 и основателем X.com, который слился с Confinity и получил имя PayPal

Main Projects

Tesla (Tesla Motors) is an American automaker, energy storage company, and solar panel manufacturer based in Palo Alto, California.

Neuralink - is an American neurotechnology company founded by Elon Musk and eight others, reported to be developing implantable brain–computer interfaces (BCIs). The company's headquarters are in San Francisco; it was started in 2016 and was first publicly reported in March 2017. The trademark "Neuralink" was purchased from its previous owners in January 2017.

PayPal Holdings - is an American company operating a worldwide online payments system that supports online money transfers and serves as an electronic alternative to traditional paper methods like checks and money orders. PayPal is one of the world's largest Internet payment companies. The company operates as a payment processor for online vendors, auction sites and other commercial users, for which it charges a fee.

Space Exploration Technologies, doing business as SpaceX, is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transport services company headquartered in Hawthorne, California. It was founded in 2002 by entrepreneur Elon Musk with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.SpaceX has since developed the Falcon launch vehicle family and the Dragon spacecraft family, which both currently deliver payloads into Earth orbit.
